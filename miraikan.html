Miraikan

The National Museum of Emerging Science and Innovation, known as the Miraikan 未来館,features many science and technological innovations. There are interactive displays within three permanent exhibits, focused on space, the earth, and sustainability. It includes a large,impressive, LED panel display of the earth (theGeo-Cosmos) with current weather patterns depicted. You'll see a demonstration of Honda’sASIMO robot, which has opposable thumbs andcan walk, run, and kick a soccer ball. There are English audio guides for the permanent exhibits and a dome theater. There is a small admission fee. Inside is a cafe and museum shop.

5-minute walk from “Tokyo International CruiseTerminal Station”

2-3-6 Aomi, Koto-ku, Tokyo, Japan
