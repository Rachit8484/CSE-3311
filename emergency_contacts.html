110 For Police (Crime and Accidents)

To call the police in Japan, 110 is the number to report an accident or crime.


119 For Fire and Emergency Services

119 is the number to call if there is a fire or some kind of medical emergency involved


118 For Coast Guard

118 is the number to call to report an incident far out in sea
